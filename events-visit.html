Parking at Ellerslie

We offer free parking to our clients & their guests

Our clients & their guests are directed to use Carpark A which is adjacent to the southern motorway and can be accessed through our vehicle entrance at Gate 3.

While this carpark is advertised as being 'pay and display', Ellerslie Event Centre clients & their guests DO NOT need to pay if they park there - the parking is complimentary to them.

If you have any concerns on the day of your event, please visit the Auckland Racing Club administration office (one-storey red brick building on your left as you walk through the main gates) and discuss with our team. The office is open 8.30am – 5.00pm weekdays.
Alternatively, call us on 09 522 3852.

Don't forget to communicate this message out to your event attendees.
